Registration to the Satellite

Learn more ▼

Register

Satellite participants will have to register to the main conference, following the procedure described in the conference website.
Participants who only wish to participate to the satellite and do not wish to attend the main conference will have the option to register only for a single day. More information will be published soon.

Abstract submission

Details on how to submit an abstract for a contributed talk during the Satellite will be available shortly. Abstract submission will be handled through EasyChair.
