Register

Satellite participants will have to register to the main conference, following the procedure described in the conference website.
Participants who only wish to participate to the satellite and do not wish to attend the main conference will have the option to register only for a single day.

Abstract submission

Abstract submission is now open with extended deadline the 12th of September, 2021. Submitted abstract will be considered for contributed talks during the Satellite. You can submit you abstract through EasyChair here (we <do not require the authors to upload a PDF paper, so the related field in the submission form can be left empty)!
